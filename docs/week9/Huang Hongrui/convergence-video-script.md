## 👔 Dress Code Suggestion

**Option A (recommended)**: Smart casual — collared shirt / blouse, no logos, solid colors
**Option B**: Matching team T-shirts (if you have time to print)

> Avoid: busy patterns, all-black, all-white (camera exposure issues)

---

## 🎥 Filming Tips

| Tip | Why |
|-----|-----|
| Stand in a quiet room | Background noise ruins audio |
| Face a window or light source | Avoid backlit (dark faces) |
| Camera at eye level | Don't look down at the phone |
| Speak 20% slower than normal | Nervousness makes you rush |
| Hold the card STILL | 3 seconds minimum for editors |
| Look at the CAMERA, not the screen | Eye contact = engagement |
| One person count down "3-2-1" | Synchronize the group tagline |

---

## ✅ Pre-Recording Checklist

- [ ] Information card written (thick marker, legible from 2m away)
- [ ] Script printed or memorized
- [ ] Representative speaker rehearsed (3+ run-throughs)
- [ ] Each member knows their one line
- [ ] Tagline practiced in unison (×5 times)
- [ ] Quiet room booked / found
- [ ] Phone/camera charged, storage available
- [ ] Test recording done (check audio + lighting)

---

> **Best take strategy**: Use Take 1 first. If anyone stumbles, switch to Take 2 (shorter, harder to mess up). Use Take 3 only if you want a different vibe.
