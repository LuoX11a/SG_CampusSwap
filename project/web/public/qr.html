Scan to open on your phone

Public URL — accessible from anywhere
  1. Open your phone's camera and scan the QR code
  2. Tap the link to open in your browser
  3. Tap Share → Add to Home Screen to install as app
Troubleshooting:
If the URL shows "localhost", open this page using your computer's IP address.
Run in PowerShell: ipconfig and look for the IPv4 under WLAN.
